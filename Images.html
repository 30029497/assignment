The Cathedral of the Blessed Sacrament
is located in the city centre of Christchurch, New Zealand
It is the mother church of the Roman Catholic Diocese of Christchurch
It is popularly known as the Christchurch Basilica

In the center of the North Island Located a largest
lake of New Zealand named Lake Taupo and Tongariro National Park,
North Island.Tongariro is a land of dramatic beauty, with towering
volcanoes.A highlight of the park is the Tongariro Alpine Crossing.

On the tumultuous Pacific Ring of Fire, Rotorua is one of the
most active geothermal regions in the world.This is a land where the
earth speaks. Boiling mud pools, hissing geysers, volcanic craters, and
steaming thermal springs betray the forces that birthed much of New Zealand's dramatic topography.

The heart of the Southern Alps, New Zealand's highest peaks rise
above the alpine landscapes of Aoraki National Park, also called Mount Cook
National ParkMount Cook Village is a great base for exploring the park and organizing
activities such as scenic flights, ski touring, heli-skiing, hunting, hiking, and stargazing trips.

Lake Wakatipu, Queenstown is New Zealand's adventure capital and one of the country's top
destinations for international visitors.Bungee jumping, jet boating, white water rafting, paragliding,
rock climbing, mountain biking, and downhill skiing are just some of the adrenaline-fueled things to do here,
and visitors can explore the stunning alpine scenery on the excellent network of hiking trails.

A World Heritage Site, Fiordland National Park protects some of the most spectacular scenery in the country.
Glaciers sculpted this dramatic landscape, carving the famous fjords of Milford, Dusky, and Doubtful Sounds.
Visitors here can explore gushing cascades, offshore islands, virgin rain forests, vast lakes, and craggy mountain peaks
